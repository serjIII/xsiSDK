Shadow

The Shadow object represents a new shadow hierarchy (a chain or null hierarchy) on top of an existing chain. Shadow chains are pose-constrained to the original objects. Shadow spines are created and pose-constrained for each vertebra in the spine.

Returned by

MakeShadowChain

MakeShadowSpine
