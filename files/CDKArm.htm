Arm

The Arm object represents a two bone arm and an up vector control object. The arm bones are created from a collection of guide objects, which should contain objects for shoulder, elbow, and hand positions respectively. A bicep bone connects the shoulder and elbow objects, and a forearm bone connects the elbow and hand objects.
