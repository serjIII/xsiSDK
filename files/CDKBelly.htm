Belly

The Belly object represents a rig consisting of two springs attached to a rectangular rod. The rod is a one-bone chain created using two guide objects. The guide objects determine the top and bottom position of the rod. One spring connects the base of the rod to the given hip object, the other spring connects the top of the rod to the vertebra object.
